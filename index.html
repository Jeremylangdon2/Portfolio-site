About me

My name is Jeremy, I am a UX designer, researcher, and product strategist. I have been doing UX for almost 2 years now. I am a Visual communications student at San Francisco State University. I enjoy data science and research. I also enjoy designing, but my main focus is on research. I have spent time doing research for companies, and I have also done work on product strategy, helping businesses and teams define their goals, while clairifying/communicating objectives as a scrum master and product owner. I enjoy working with clients and getting to know them. I enjoy the process of helping them find what they want, and building it. In my free time I like to play the piano, compose classical music, learn to code, and hangout with my cat Bob. I live in Marin county, and enjoy living near the ocean. Most days you will either find me working or at school.

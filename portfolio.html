About Tech Fleet Projects

Hands-on Experience

Tech Fleet projects give practical experience on real client work across UX, product, and dev—and is set up to mirror how teams ship in the real world.

Agile Methodology

Teams follow Agile in short sprints to deliver incremental value while learning quickly from each cycle.

Cross-functional Teams

Teams collaborate in diverse, cross-disciplinary squads—similar to how product orgs are structured.

Learning Environment

Mentorship-driven space where experimentation is encouraged and mistakes are treated as learning opportunities.

Client-Based Work

Projects involve real clients—typically nonprofits—so participants collaborate with external stakeholders and deliver work with real-world impact, not self-promotion.

Community-Driven

The community collaborates on project selection, team formation, and mentorship—learning from peers and co-leads.

Focus on Impact

Projects aim to benefit apprentices and clients, emphasizing technology that serves people and purpose.
